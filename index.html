Instruction

How To Play This Game:

1. Players take turns rolling the dice.

2. Players may roll the dice as many times as they like on their turn.

3. Players may declare "Hold" at any time during their turn. By declaring "Hold", that player gets the points up to that point and the turn moves to the next player.

4. If player roll "1", then there Current Points will become 0 and other player rolls.

5. If the total roll is less than 100 points, the player gets a point.

6. If the score exceeds 100 points, the player wins.
